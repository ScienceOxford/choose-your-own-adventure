Allow your reader to customise the story

You can use inputs to allow your reader to e.g. give the character a name.


Step 1: Creating a set-up passage.

Asking your reader to input information pops up a box over your story - it does not work neatly within your paragraph.

To keep the flow of the story, you can start your story with a new passage, that asks your reader for their name before the story officially starts.

Click the +Passage button on the bottom left to create a new, unconnected, passage.

Copy and paste the below code into this passage, then close the passage.

(set: $name to (prompt: "Hello, what is your name?", ""))
Welcome to the story $name!

This does a few things:

  • Sets a new variable $name
  • Uses a prompt to ask the reader for some information
  • Uses the newly created variable in a sentence

Test out the code by hovering over the passage, and clicking the play button.


Edit the code so that it fits your story.

  • What do you want the pop up to say?
  • What happens when you put something inside the blank speech marks "" at the end of the first line?

Step 2: Connect this passage to your story

Once you have finished writing your passage, you can connect it to the start of your story.

To do this, you need to know the title of your opening passage, in my example it is Beginning.

Instead of asking the reader to click on a box called Beginning, I am going to call my link something else, but point it to the correct passage.

[[Start your Adventure ->Beginning]]

Add the code to your passage, and edit it so that Beginning is the title of your first passage.

Check that your set-up passage now has an arrow linking it to your story.


Finally, you need to change the official start of your story to your new set-up passage.

Hover over the passage, and click the three dots ... to access the menu. Click Start Story Here. The green rocket will now be on the top left corner of the passage.


If you have asked your reader for their name, you should use it in your story! Look through all of your paragraphs, and add $name to put their name into the text.

What other questions could you ask your reader? How will you use this information throughout your story?
